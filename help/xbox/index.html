Steredenn - Help

You have one mission in Steredenn: survive as long as you can.

Control your ship with:

Move
Shoot
Pick bonus
Swap weapons

Your other goal, besides surviving, is to use your weapons to destroy anything that comes close to you. Be fast, and you will get more points.

You can follow a special training that will teach you everything you need to know about the game. Select "Play" in the main menu and choose "Tutorial":

Be ready, space wanderer.
