Steredenn

Steredenn is a roguelike-shmup video game for PC/Mac/Linux, Xbox One, PlayStation 4 and iPhone/iPad made by Pixelnest Studio.

Steredenn is a frenetic and chaotic space shooter, carved in big beautiful pixels, with insane boss battles. Embark in your ship and engage the fight against dreadful space pirates in a never-ending combat for your survival.

The game is fast, addictive, hard and action-packed.

Factsheet

What

Four modes (normal, daily run, arena, superplay), 13 terrific bosses, special events, 30 environments, 25 upgrades for your spaceship, 35 weapons and hundreds of enemies waves.

Available in English, Japanese, French, Italian, German, Spanish, Russian, Korean, Brazilian Portuguese, Chinese, Breton.

Who

The game is designed and developed by Damien Mayance and Matthieu Oger of Pixelnest Studio.

Made in collaboration with Plus (arts), Etienne Marque (audio) and Zander Noriega (music).
