Steredenn?

Steredenn is an upcoming (video game) shooter developed by Pixelnest Studio and Plus.

Steredenn is a frenetic space shooter, carved in big beautiful pixels. Embark in your ship and engage the fight against dreadful space pirates in a never-ending run to make the biggest score!

In Steredenn, each new enemy you will encounter is bigger and stronger than the previous ones. But, fear not! Your weapons are as equally sized. LAUNCH. ENGAGE. CRUSH!
