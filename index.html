Steredenn?

Steredenn is an upcoming video game developed by Pixelnest Studio and Plus.

Steredenn is a frenetic and chaotic space shooter, carved in big beautiful pixels, with insane boss battles. Embark in your ship and engage the fight against dreadful space pirates in a never-ending combat for your survival.

You are one of the last. Your mothership has been destroyed and your teammates are far, far away. You will have to fight to survive! How long can you stand against an endless army of fierce enemies? They won't stop until your dying breath.

Steredenn is at the crossroads between a shmup and a rogue-like. Enjoy the infinite replayability of the game with its unforgiving gameplay, intense boss fights, (almost-)randomly generated levels, twisted space events and a surprisingly large arsenal of weapons.

You won't let down your gamepad that easily. ;)
